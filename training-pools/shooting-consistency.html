Consistent shooting is the foundation of any great Rocket League bot. This training pack focuses on developing your bot's ability to place accurate and powerful shots from various positions on the field.

Training Tips

  1. Focus on Accuracy First - Aim for specific corners before adding power
  2. Vary Your Approach - Practice from different angles and distances
  3. Watch the Defender - Learn to read the goalie's position
  4. Use the Right Shot - Different situations call for different types of shots
  5. Be Patient - Sometimes waiting for the perfect moment is better than rushing
