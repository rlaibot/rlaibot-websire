Master the Walls

Wall play is an essential skill for any advanced Rocket League bot. This training pack focuses on developing your bot's ability to read and control the ball off the walls, make effective clears, and execute powerful shots from difficult angles.

Training Focus Areas

Wall Clears

Practice powerful and accurate clears from your own back wall to relieve pressure and start counter-attacks.

  • Basic clears to midfield
  • Dribble setups
  • Passing plays

Wall Shots

Develop the ability to read and shoot the ball effectively from the walls.

  • Direct shots on goal
  • Backboard reads
  • Angle shots

Wall Defense

Learn to defend against wall plays and make crucial saves.

  • Shadow defense
  • Wall challenges
  • Backboard clears

Training Tips

  1. Positioning is Key - Learn to position your car parallel to the wall for better control
  2. Ball Reading - Practice predicting the ball's trajectory off the wall
  3. Boost Management - Learn when to use boost for wall plays
  4. Recovery - Master landing on the wall and transitioning back to the ground
  5. Power vs. Control - Know when to hit the ball hard versus when to maintain possession
